About Me


I'm Glen Martin Lau, a Filipino-Chinese, who was born in the Philippines. I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Electronics and Communications Engineering from the University of Santo Tomas. I am a programmer/ analyst with 7 years of experience on Mainframe and 2 years on AS/400. I am also enhancing my skills by learning full stack web development. I am an introvert and I believe that nothing is impossible to reach as long as I believe in in what I do and what I can do to achieve my dreams.

My Humble Beginnings

My IT Career


I started my IT career as a mainframe programmer trainee at Software Ventures International, handling offshore production support roles for Northwest Airlines from 2007 to 2008. I then moved to US Technology Global and became a software engineer, working on the Wellpoint Dental project between 2008 and 2010. I then worked for QBE Management Services as a programmer/ analyst to develop and support the system of their Australian region from 2010 to 2013 and the e-business project of QBE Asia until its completion in 2014.

After a long and productive seven-year career in IT, I was intrigued by the booming e-commerce industry, so I've decided to join growing companies LazadaPH and ShopeePH as an entrepreneur and a business owner. I've opened some online businesses between 2014 and 2017. In 2017, following the success of my shop brands, I, together with some business partners, started an arts and crafts cafe and restaurant, wherein we created a collaborative business model for small entrepreneurs to showcase their items onsite and act as an event hub for them.

My Entrepreneurship

My New Beginnings


After seeing the volatility of physical shops during the pandemic, I've realized that the world is shifting to the digital era nowadays with the sudden influx of fintech and AI. It made me appreciate the beauty of technology and brought out "the programmer" in me. This then gives me ample time to extend my programming knowledge. By being a full stack developer, this gives me a new beginning, a new opportunity, and a new chapter in my life.
