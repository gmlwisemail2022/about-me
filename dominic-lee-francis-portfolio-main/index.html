Who am I?

In this leadership capacity I guided a talented team but also spearheaded curriculum planning and managed the school's vibrant social media platforms.


Simultaneously, I am enrolled in an accelerated full-stack web development bootcamp, where I am immersing myself in the latest technologies and methodologies. This intensive program in empowering me with a robust foundation in web development, laying the groundwork for exciting future projects. I am poised to contribute innovatively to the dynamic landscape of web development.
